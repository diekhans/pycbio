
based on http://www.python.org/doc/essays/styleguide.html:

    * _single_leading_underscore - module internal
    * __double_leading_underscore - class-private names in Python 1.4.
    * single_trailing_underscore - avoid conflicts with Python keyword
    * __double_leading_and_trailing_underscore__ = "magic" objects or
      attributes that live in user-controlled namespaces
